Futile Body

by grimeyluv

This corpse of mine drags around
from bed to school, it grinds on the pavement
little by little, it shaves off my weight.
I don't like this body,
it's heavy and needy,
always asking for food while high on worries
like a tank without fuel, occupied by a baby.
Time will pass as this body rots, slowly but surely
my once smooth skin will droop to the ground
and I will thaw into non-existing.
